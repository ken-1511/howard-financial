Who We Are

About HowardFinancial

Since 2015

This platform is being developed as a dApp (decentralized app) to foster the next generation of real estate transactions.

At Howard Financial, we envision a uniquely American opportunity at the intersection of real estate and cryptocurrency.

We proudly serve the communities of Alton, IL and St. Louis, Missouri. David Howard, principal founder, brings each unique property to life, carefully curating dream estates complete with luxurious modern comforts while honoring the charming Midwest-Americana heritage.

Currently based out of Southern California, our father, mother, and sons company hopes to bring warmth and hearth to you and your loved ones from one family to another.

David Howard

Principal Founder
